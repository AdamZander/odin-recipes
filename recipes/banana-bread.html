Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is always good on a cool fall day! Goes well with whipped cream or icecream also.

Ingredients

  • White or wholegrain flour
  • Very ripe bananas
  • Almonds (if desired)
  • Surger

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in bowl
  2. Add bananas
  3. Add all to bread pan
  4. Bank at 180C until done

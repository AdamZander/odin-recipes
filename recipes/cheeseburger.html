Grilled Cheeseburger

Description

This is the way I grill my cheeseburgers! I think grilling should be kept simple! Good quality hamburger is very important

Ingredients

  • Good quality hamburger, 280gr for 2 hamburgers
  • Cheeder cheese
  • Salt and pepper. I like to use a pepper mix.
  • Hamburger buns, your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat grill, must be very hot!
  2. Add a good amount of salt and pepper to both sides of hamburger
  3. Grill hamburgers 5 minutes per side, or until desired color
  4. Grill buns lightly
  5. Once cooked, put hamburgers in a place to keep warm, add cheese on top
  6. Once cheese is melted put in bun and serve with desired sauce
